Diffusers

Essential oil diffusers are one of the most popular ways to experience aromatherapy. Essential oils are combined with water and dispersed into the air using ultrasonic technology, filling the room with their therapeutic benefits. Renegade Wellness Center carries a variety of styles, from hand blown glass and ceramic designer options to sleek and portable travel models.
