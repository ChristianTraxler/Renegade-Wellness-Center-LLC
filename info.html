More Info

Halotherapy is an inhalation of kinetically activated pure dry salt particles. Dry salt is super absorbent and antibacterial. These micro sized salt particles travel deep into the respiratory system and absorb excess moisture, remove allergens, toxins, foreign substances, break up stagnated mucus, restore the function of cilia and widen the airways for better lung function and more efficient oxygen distribution. Halotherapy is also topically beneficial and can help manage many skin conditions.


Halotherapy may provide relief from the following conditions:

• Allergies
• Asthma
• Bronchitis
• Bronchial Infections
• COPD
• Cold & Flu
• Cystic Fibrosis
• Emphysema
• Pneumonia
• Rhinitis
• Sinusitis
• Smoker's Cough
• Wheezing
• Snoring (in some cases)
• Acne
• Dermatitis
• Dry/Flaky Skin
• Eczema
• Psoriasis
• Rash
• Rosacea
• Skin Aging
• Inflammation
• Stress
• Lack of Focus
• Low Energy
• Sleep Disturbances


Salt therapy is appropriate for adults and children 6 months and older. Salinity level can be adjusted for young children and those who are more sensitive.


*Please note that while many symptoms can be relieved from the above list of ailments you should not have Halotherapy while issues are acute. This means that when seeking relief for cold, flu, etc. you should wait until you have not had a fever for at least 48 hours and are not experiencing active symptoms. Please observe the appropriate waiting period as advised by your doctor for specific illnesses before making an appointment.

**Although published studies indicate that Salt Care has health benefits the research supporting its use has not been filed with the FDA for approval.
