Aromatics International

Aromatics International provides high quality essential oils, hydrosols, and carriers. They are completely transparent which means providing GC/MS reports for each batch of oils as well as country of origin and production date. They only work with farmers, distillers, and producers who grow plants in their native environments using traditional practices which is necessary for therapeutic effectiveness. You will find our current offering of their products here but if there is a favorite essential oil or other item that you love we would be happy to stock it for you, just ask!
