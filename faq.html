Infrared

Infrared is a safe and gentle wavelength in the natural spectrum of light. It penetrates human tissue and the energy it delivers is just the right nudge cells need to stimulate warmth, growth, and release. Infrared saunas emit lightwaves that penetrate the body to stimulate health benefits from within.

Traditional saunas heat the air to a point that initiates the body’s natural cooling process. This means that blood comes to the surface of the skin and opens the pores.

Infrared saunas emit safe lightwaves that penetrate the body to stimulate the health benefits from within at a cellular level and allow for a deeper detox.

Renegade Wellness Center has chosen a far infrared sauna bed. This allows for a private, comfortable experience and allows you to keep your face out of the direct heat. Since your face tends to get hot first, keeping it out of the sauna bed allows your body to tolerate longer sessions and therefore deeper detox and better health benefits.

Your session will total 45 minutes which includes 25-40 minutes in the sauna depending on your preference and the rest of the time spent as cool down time in a relaxing environment. A bottle of alkaline or regular water will be provided so that you can rehydrate. Your session will also include rose water mist and cooling pads for the eyes or forehead applied 15 minutes into your session if you like.
