Authentic Himalayan salt lamps are natural air purifiers that come straight from the foot hills of the Himalayan Mountains. This is the only place in the world where this type of salt comes from. When warmed, Himalayan salt emits negative ions which have a relaxing effect on the body. Their warm glow also helps relax the eyes from the overuse of electronic devices. Lamps from The Original Salt Company are hand carved into beautiful designs and each one has a dimmable light switch so that you can adjust the brightness if you would like.
