Jojoba Oil

$18

/4oz

One of our most popular carriers! Jojoba’s molecular stability makes it a favorite for blending, and it’s subtle, barely-there aroma allows the scents of your essential oils to shine. Since jojoba resembles the natural oil produced by our skin (sebum), it’s easily absorbed, delivering luxurious hydration and antioxidants without clogging pores. Our certified organic jojoba is cold pressed from the beans of shrub-like plants in the southern regions of the United States. While jojoba is often referred to as oil, it’s actually a liquid wax.
