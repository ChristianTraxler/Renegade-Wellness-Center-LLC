Carrier Oils

Jojoba Oil

$18.00- 4oz

One of our most popular carriers! Jojoba’s molecular stability makes it a favorite for blending, and it’s subtle, barely-there aroma allows the scents of your essential oils to shine. Since jojoba resembles the natural oil produced by our skin (sebum), it’s easily absorbed, delivering luxurious hydration and antioxidants without clogging pores. Our certified organic jojoba is cold pressed from the beans of shrub-like plants in the southern regions of the United States. While jojoba is often referred to as oil, it’s actually a liquid wax.
Mango Kernel Butter

$26.00- 4oz

An exotic, wildcrafted butter beloved in tropical regions and rainforests, mango kernel butter is luxuriously emollient—softening and soothing the skin. Its aroma is mild, with a trace of nuts. Use mango kernel butter in blends to soothe chronic dryness, protect skin from weather (such as the wind and sun), restore a supple texture to mature skin, and improve skin’s overall tone. A little mango kernel butter goes a long way when blended with other natural oils and butters. Our unrefined mango kernel butter is cold pressed in India.
Trauma Oil

$18.00- 1oz

The ultimate oil for recovery blends! Our house-made Trauma Oil is profoundly comforting for muscles, joints, the head, and skin. Use it alone, or blend in your favorite essential oils for both urgent and ongoing issues. Our Trauma Oil is a powerhouse blend of three herbally infused oils: St. John’s wort, calendula, and arnica. All three infusions are handcrafted with fresh flowers (either wildcrafted or organically grown), and organic, virgin, first-pressed olive oil.
